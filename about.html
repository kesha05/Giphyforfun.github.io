Giphy is an online database and search engine that allows users to search for and share short looping videos with no sound, that resemble animated GIF files.

The fastest and easiest way to bring the full GIPHY experience directly to your app is with GIPHY SDK.

Built with developers and product designers in mind, GIPHY SDK is a top-to-bottom solution for all things GIF in your app. This includes interfacing with GIPHY API, fetching and caching assets, and displaying GIFs and Stickers on screen in customizable UI templates.
